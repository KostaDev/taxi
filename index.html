Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a ride?

The best way to reserve a ride is to contact us directly via Viber, WhatsApp, or Telegram, or send us an email at zoran.u.jovanovic@gmail.com. Our team is available 24/7 and will confirm your reservation promptly.

How is the price calculated?

The price is calculated based on the type of service and the duration of the ride. For a precise quote tailored to your journey, please contact us directly and we will be happy to assist.

Are pets allowed?

All small and medium-sized pets are welcome in our vehicles. Please note that pet transport is subject to an additional charge. We kindly ask that pets be kept in a carrier where possible.
